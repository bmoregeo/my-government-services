Online Help:

The City of New Orleans Planning and Zoning Lookup Tool helps residents identify Zoning and Conditional Use information. This application can be used on a desktop computer, mobile phone, or tablet device.

Finding an Area of Interest

There are three primary ways to identify an area of interest:
1. Search by address.
2. You can use your current location if this funciionality is enabled on your mobile device.
3. You can interact directly with the map and click anywhere withinin Orleans Parish for information.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:

1. Use the basemap switcher to select the basemap you'd like to interact with
2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
3. Hold Down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out
5. Drag the zoom slider to zoom in/out

Searching by Address

The search box can be used to find an address:

1. Enter the address you're interested in
2. Click on the item in the list to locate the address on the map

Zoning and Conditional Use Information

After searching for a respective address or clicking on the map, relevant Zoning and Conditional Use (if applicable) will be presented in the bottom of the application.

Map Layers

You can toggle certain Enterprise GIS Map Layers on or off by opening the Layer control. These layers are turned off by default, but can be turned on when the map is zoomed into to prescribed zoom scales.
